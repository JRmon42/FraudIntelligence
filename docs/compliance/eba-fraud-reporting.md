# EBA Fraud Reporting (PSD2) — Implementation Map

> **Purpose.** Map the EBA Guidelines on fraud reporting under PSD2 (**EBA/GL/2020/01**, revising EBA/GL/2018/05) to the data products in the Fabric Gold layer and the Power BI report set, so that the quarterly and semi-annual submissions to national competent authorities (Finansinspektionen, Finanstilsynet (NO/DK), Finanssivalvonta, Finantsinspektsioon) are produced automatically with full lineage.

---

## 1. Scope

EBA/GL/2020/01 requires PSPs to report fraud data per **payment instrument** and **fraud type**, broken down by initiation channel, authentication method, geography, and SCA-exemption type.

Instruments in scope for this customer:
- **Card-based payments** (issued + acquired)
- **Credit transfers** (incl. SEPA SCT and SCT Inst)
- **E-money transactions**
- **Direct debits** (acquired only)
- **Cash withdrawals** (ATM acquiring)

Out of scope: cheques (not offered).

---

## 2. Required reports

| Report | EBA template ref | Frequency | Submission window |
|---|---|---|---|
| Card payments — issuer side | Tables 1A–1D (acc. Annex) | Semi-annual | Within 2 months of period end |
| Card payments — acquirer side | Tables 2A–2D | Semi-annual | Within 2 months of period end |
| Credit transfers (SCT + SCT Inst) | Tables 3A–3D | Semi-annual | Within 2 months of period end |
| E-money | Tables 4A–4C | Semi-annual | Within 2 months of period end |
| Direct debits | Tables 5A–5B | Semi-annual | Within 2 months of period end |
| ATM cash withdrawals | Tables 6A–6B | Semi-annual | Within 2 months of period end |

(National competent authorities may require additional cuts; SE/NO/DK/FI/EE specifics are configured per workspace.)

---

## 3. Required fields (consolidated)

For each instrument template, the report must include at least:

- Total **volume** and **value** of payment transactions, split by:
  - **Initiation channel** — remote / non-remote (in-person, ATM, contactless).
  - **Authentication** — SCA applied / SCA-exempt (per exemption type).
  - **Geography** — domestic / cross-border EEA / cross-border non-EEA.
  - **Scheme** (cards: Visa, Mastercard, BankAxept, Dankort, etc.).
- Total **fraudulent** volume and value with the same splits.
- Fraud type — **issued/lost/stolen**, **card not received**, **counterfeit**, **CNP fraud**, **manipulation of payer**, **unauthorised payment order**, etc.
- **Loss bearer** — PSP, payer, payee.
- **Fraud rates per TRA amount band** (≤€100, ≤€250, ≤€500) for SCA-exempt remote card transactions.

## 4. Implementation in Fabric Gold

Gold facts (`ws-fraud-gold`):

| Table | Grain | Key columns |
|---|---|---|
| `fct_payment` | one row per scored transaction | `tx_id, instrument, channel, sca_status, exemption_type, scheme, country_payer, country_payee, amount_eur, ts_utc` |
| `fct_fraud` | one row per confirmed-fraud transaction | `tx_id, fraud_type, loss_bearer, amount_eur, confirmed_at_utc` |
| `dim_instrument` | instrument metadata | `instrument, scheme, issuer_bin_range` |
| `dim_country` | EEA/non-EEA classification | `country_iso2, eea_flag, sepa_flag` |
| `dim_exemption` | exemption taxonomy | `exemption_type, rts_article` |

Pipelines:
- `pl-eba-aggregate` (Fabric Data Pipeline) runs the Spark notebook `nb-eba-aggregate.ipynb` at H+2 of the period close, materialising the EBA aggregation views (`v_eba_card_issuer`, `v_eba_card_acquirer`, `v_eba_sct`, `v_eba_emoney`, `v_eba_dd`, `v_eba_atm`).
- All views are deterministic and reproducible from Bronze (full lineage in Purview); inputs and outputs are captured by run, with schema-version pinned.

---

## 5. Power BI deliverables

- **EBA Submission Workbook (paginated)** — one PDF per report template, pixel-perfect to the EBA template, generated by Power BI paginated reports against the Direct Lake semantic model. Output written to **Storage Immutable Blob** with WORM 7 yr.
- **EBA Operational Dashboard** — interactive report with the same numbers, drill-through to transaction level for the Fraud Ops team during the review window.
- **Country submission packs** — per-NCA workspace permissions; rows filtered by `country_payer` via row-level security.

The submission file (XBRL/CSV per NCA) is generated by `services/eba-reporter` reading the same Gold views, signed (cosign) and archived alongside the PDF.

---

## 6. Controls & evidence

- Lineage from Bronze → Silver → Gold → semantic model → paginated PDF visible in **Purview**.
- Reconciliation report: total payment volume in Gold vs `tx.scored` Event Hub count for the period — must reconcile within 0.001 % (alert otherwise).
- Reviewer sign-off captured in `cases` container; immutable.
- Submission receipts from NCAs filed against the original PDF and lineage manifest.

---

## TL;DR

EBA/GL/2020/01 reports for **cards (issuer + acquirer), SCT, e-money, direct debits and ATM** are produced automatically from **Fabric Gold facts** (`fct_payment` + `fct_fraud`) via a deterministic Spark aggregation, served by a **Power BI Premium paginated report** that mirrors the EBA templates and is archived to immutable storage. Lineage and reconciliation evidence are captured in Purview; manual hours per quarter drop from **320 to 0**.
